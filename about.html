OUR STORY

Empowering women through innovative activewear that combines performance, style, and sustainability. Discover the journey that brought Cropella to life.

OUR MISSION

At Cropella, we believe that every woman deserves to feel confident, comfortable, and empowered in her active lifestyle. Our mission is to create premium activewear that not only performs at the highest level but also makes you look and feel amazing.

We're committed to designing pieces that seamlessly transition from the gym to everyday life, ensuring that style and functionality go hand in hand. Every garment is crafted with attention to detail, using innovative fabrics and construction techniques that enhance your performance while maintaining our signature aesthetic.

HOW IT STARTED

Founded in 2020, Cropella was born from a simple yet powerful idea: activewear should be as beautiful as it is functional. Our founder, a fitness enthusiast and fashion lover, was frustrated by the lack of stylish, high-performance activewear that could keep up with her active lifestyle.

What began as a small collection of leggings and sports bras has grown into a comprehensive activewear brand that women across Indonesia trust for their daily workouts and active pursuits. We've stayed true to our roots while continuously innovating and expanding our offerings.
